ABOUT

FOSS CEAL is a part of International Centre for Free and Open Source Software, enhancing the Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) ecosystem.

Introducing FOSS CEAL (Free and Open Source Software Centre of Excellence Attingal) - a dynamic force within the College of Engineering Attingal, committed to propelling open-source technology and fostering innovation. Through pioneering projects like Wa Ceal Bot and piCloud Self Hosting Server etc., we exemplify the transformative potential of open-source solutions in tackling real-world challenges. Aligned with the International Centre for Free and Open Source Software (ICFOSS), FOSS CEAL collaborates with esteemed industry partners, academic institutions, and open-source communities to facilitate collaboration, share knowledge, and drive impactful change within the technology landscape.
